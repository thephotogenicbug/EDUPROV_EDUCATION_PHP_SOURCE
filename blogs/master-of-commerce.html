Master of Commerce

Master of Commerce / M.Com commonly known as M. Com is a postgraduate program in Commerce, finance, economics and accounts related aspects. The duration of this master’s degree course is 2 years. Upon satisfying the eligibility criteria one can know further about how to join in this course and what are the advantages of being an M. Com graduate.On successful completion of the coursework, the candidate will be honored with a postgraduate degree of M. Com.

In this course, students learn the skills like corporate management and marketing, business responsibilities and Finance management, etc. that make them job ready right after the course completion. The candidates can work in the fields of human resource, research and development, accounting, finance and many more.

For this coursework, the candidates are required to choose one or two specializations as per their interest areas. You have options to choose from various business subjects. While following the coursework, you study about diverse topics and subjects such as Corporate Accounting, Business Economics, Financial and Service Management, Tax Management and Planning, Marketing and Entrepreneurship etc.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for M.Com coursework, the aspirants must have a graduation degree in Commerce. Both modes, direct admissions and through entrance exam are available for admission in this course. Colleges and universities providing direct admissions usually demand 60% marks in the graduation course. reas, the admission process through entrance exam purely depends on the aspirant’s merits in the said entrance exam.
