Master of Computer Application

Master of Computer Application / MCA is a postgraduate course in Computer Science. The duration of this course is 3 years and it covers various topics from information technology, business management, and mathematics. This coursework has been designed for people that want to advance their careers as computer science professionals. MCA students can also specialize in fields like Hardware Technology, Software Development, System Engineering, System Development, Application software. On successful completion of this course, candidates will be honored with a master degree in Computer Application.

This master degree program includes the study of various advanced aspects of computer science discipline. The coursework focuses on real-world problem solving by computer programs, computer software designing, coding and database systems. Also, the course includes the study of multimedia, network and information security, software testing, etc. In this Modern Era, Computer science professionals are in high demand so, on the successful completion of the course, students have diverse career options to choose from. Either they can become software programmers, testers, database administrator or professional IT analysts.

Eligibility Criteria

The basic eligibility for the MCA coursework is a bachelor degree in any Discipline with Mathematics in higher secondary board examination. The aspirants will have to qualify the entrance exam to get the admission in this course and admission will be purely on the basis of the merits of the mentioned exam.
